This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of food. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Food

### Ideas Bank

- favorite dish and why
- home-cooked meals vs eating out
- healthy eating habits
- popular meals in your country
- how your taste changed with age

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- What is your favourite food?
- Is there any food you dislike?
- Do you have a healthy diet?
- What do you think of fast food?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: What is your favourite food?

Model answer: I would describe it as practical and genuinely useful. Personally, cooking simple healthy meals during the week.

#### Q2: Is there any food you dislike?

Model answer: Overall, yes, in most situations. A good example is cooking simple healthy meals during the week.

#### Q3: Do you have a healthy diet?

Model answer: In short, yes. In day-to-day life, cooking simple healthy meals during the week.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
