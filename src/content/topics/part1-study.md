This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of study. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Study

### Ideas Bank

- your subject and why you chose it
- your daily study routine
- parts you enjoy and difficult parts
- how study has changed since school
- your career plans after graduation

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- What do you study?
- Why did you choose that subject?
- Do you like that subject?
- Do you plan to get a job in the same field as your subject?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: What do you study?

Model answer: I would describe it as practical and genuinely useful. Personally, planning assignments and preparing for exam weeks.

#### Q2: Why did you choose that subject?

Model answer: The main reason is simple: it matches my strengths and future direction. In practice, it has been the most sustainable choice for me.

#### Q3: Do you like that subject?

Model answer: Yes, generally I would say so. For example, planning assignments and preparing for exam weeks.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
