This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of flowers. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Flowers

### Ideas Bank

- whether you like receiving or giving flowers
- flowers linked to specific events
- favorite flower and why
- symbolic meanings in your culture
- fresh flowers vs artificial flowers

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Do you like flowers?
- What is your favourite flower?
- When was the last time you gave someone flowers?
- Do any flowers have a special meaning in your country?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Do you like flowers?

Model answer: Yes, generally I would say so. For example, buying fresh flowers for birthdays and family visits.

#### Q2: What is your favourite flower?

Model answer: I would describe it as practical and genuinely useful. Personally, buying fresh flowers for birthdays and family visits.

#### Q3: When was the last time you gave someone flowers?

Model answer: Usually when it is convenient and easy to manage. For example, buying fresh flowers for birthdays and family visits.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
