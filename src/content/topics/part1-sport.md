This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of sport. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Sport

### Ideas Bank

- sports you enjoy watching or playing
- benefits of regular exercise
- sport habits in your country
- childhood sport experience
- ways people stay fit

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Do you like sport?
- What is your favourite sport?
- Did you play sport as a child?
- How do most people in your country keep fit?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Do you like sport?

Model answer: Overall, yes, in most situations. A good example is doing regular workouts to stay fit and manage stress.

#### Q2: What is your favourite sport?

Model answer: I would describe it as practical and genuinely useful. Personally, doing regular workouts to stay fit and manage stress.

#### Q3: Did you play sport as a child?

Model answer: Overall, yes, in most situations. A good example is doing regular workouts to stay fit and manage stress.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
