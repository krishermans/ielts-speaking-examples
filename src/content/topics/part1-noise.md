This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of noise. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Noise

### Ideas Bank

- how noisy your area is
- sounds you hear every day
- pleasant sounds vs annoying sounds
- how you find quiet time
- noise reduction in cities

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Do you live in a noisy area?
- What sounds do you hear daily?
- Where do you go to spend time in peace and quiet?
- Are there any sounds you find pleasant?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Do you live in a noisy area?

Model answer: In short, yes. In day-to-day life, avoiding peak traffic hours when my area gets loud.

#### Q2: What sounds do you hear daily?

Model answer: I see it as a sensible choice rather than a trend. In my own routine, avoiding peak traffic hours when my area gets loud.

#### Q3: Where do you go to spend time in peace and quiet?

Model answer: Mostly in situations that fit my routine. In my case, avoiding peak traffic hours when my area gets loud.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
