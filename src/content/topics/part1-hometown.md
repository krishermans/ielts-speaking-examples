This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of your hometown. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Hometown

### Ideas Bank

- where it is and what people notice first
- a place you are emotionally connected to
- what has improved over the years
- public transport and daily convenience
- what tourists can do there

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Where is your hometown?
- Do you like your hometown?
- Has your hometown changed much since you were a child?
- Do you think your hometown is a good place to bring up children?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Where is your hometown?

Model answer: I generally choose times and places that are practical. A typical example is showing visitors the old center and local market.

#### Q2: Do you like your hometown?

Model answer: Yes, generally I would say so. For example, showing visitors the old center and local market.

#### Q3: Has your hometown changed much since you were a child?

Model answer: To me, it is clearly part of daily life. A simple example is showing visitors the old center and local market.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
