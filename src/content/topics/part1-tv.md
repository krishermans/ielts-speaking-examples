This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of TV. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 TV

### Ideas Bank

- what you watch most often
- TV vs streaming habits
- programs from childhood
- foreign shows and subtitles
- how TV affects daily routines

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Do you often watch TV?
- What sorts of things do you watch on TV?
- Do you watch foreign programs or films?
- Do you think children should watch TV?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Do you often watch TV?

Model answer: In short, yes. In day-to-day life, watching documentaries and selected series in moderation.

#### Q2: What sorts of things do you watch on TV?

Model answer: I see it as a sensible choice rather than a trend. In my own routine, watching documentaries and selected series in moderation.

#### Q3: Do you watch foreign programs or films?

Model answer: Yes, generally I would say so. For example, watching documentaries and selected series in moderation.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
