This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of family and friends. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Family And Friends

### Ideas Bank

- who you spend most time with
- what quality time means to you
- how friendships changed since childhood
- family values in your country
- keeping relationships strong

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Do you spend much time with your family?
- Who are you closest to in your family?
- Are you still friends with people from your childhood?
- Is family important in your country?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Do you spend much time with your family?

Model answer: Overall, yes, in most situations. A good example is keeping regular contact through calls and weekend meetups.

#### Q2: Who are you closest to in your family?

Model answer: I would say it matters more than people think. In practical terms, keeping regular contact through calls and weekend meetups.

#### Q3: Are you still friends with people from your childhood?

Model answer: Yes, generally I would say so. For example, keeping regular contact through calls and weekend meetups.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
