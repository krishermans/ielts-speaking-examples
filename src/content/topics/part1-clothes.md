# Part 1 Clothes

### Ideas Bank

- your usual style and why
- comfort vs fashion
- where you usually shop
- traditional clothing in your country
- how your style changed over time

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Are clothes important to you?
- What kind of clothes do you usually wear?
- Where do you usually buy your clothes?
- Do most people in your country follow fashion?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Are clothes important to you?

Model answer: Overall, yes, in most situations. A good example is choosing practical outfits that still look neat.

#### Q2: What kind of clothes do you usually wear?

Model answer: I see it as a sensible choice rather than a trend. In my own routine, choosing practical outfits that still look neat.

#### Q3: Where do you usually buy your clothes?

Model answer: I generally choose times and places that are practical. A typical example is choosing practical outfits that still look neat.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
