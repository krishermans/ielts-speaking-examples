This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of childhood. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Childhood

### Ideas Bank

- your earliest memory
- games and activities you loved
- friends and social life as a child
- city vs countryside childhood
- what childhood taught you

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Did you enjoy your childhood?
- What is your first memory of your childhood?
- What did you enjoy doing as a child?
- Do you think it is better for children to grow up in the city or in the countryside?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Did you enjoy your childhood?

Model answer: Yes, generally I would say so. For example, playing outside with friends after school.

#### Q2: What is your first memory of your childhood?

Model answer: I see it as a sensible choice rather than a trend. In my own routine, playing outside with friends after school.

#### Q3: What did you enjoy doing as a child?

Model answer: For me, it is both meaningful and realistic in daily life. A clear example is playing outside with friends after school.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
