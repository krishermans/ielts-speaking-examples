This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of drinks. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Drinks

### Ideas Bank

- your favorite daily drink
- healthy vs sugary drinks
- drinks linked to culture and celebrations
- how your drink preferences changed
- hydration habits

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- What is your favourite drink?
- Is it common for people to drink tea and coffee in your country?
- Did you prefer different drinks as a child?
- What is a traditional drink in your country for celebrating?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: What is your favourite drink?

Model answer: I would describe it as practical and genuinely useful. Personally, alternating coffee in the morning and water during the day.

#### Q2: Is it common for people to drink tea and coffee in your country?

Model answer: Overall, yes, in most situations. A good example is alternating coffee in the morning and water during the day.

#### Q3: Did you prefer different drinks as a child?

Model answer: Overall, yes, in most situations. A good example is alternating coffee in the morning and water during the day.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
