This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of transport. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Transport

### Ideas Bank

- how you usually commute
- public transport quality in your area
- bus vs train preferences
- traffic challenges
- changes you want in local transport

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- How did you get here today?
- What is your favourite mode of transport?
- Do you use public transport?
- What is the difference between taking a bus and taking a train?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: How did you get here today?

Model answer: From my perspective, it is quite relevant in everyday life. For instance, combining public transport with short walks.

#### Q2: What is your favourite mode of transport?

Model answer: I would describe it as practical and genuinely useful. Personally, combining public transport with short walks.

#### Q3: Do you use public transport?

Model answer: In short, yes. In day-to-day life, combining public transport with short walks.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
