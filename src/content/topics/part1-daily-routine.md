This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of your daily routine. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Daily Routine

### Ideas Bank

- morning habits that set your day
- parts of your routine that save time
- how flexible your schedule is
- childhood routine vs present routine
- small routines that improve wellbeing

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- When do you usually get up in the morning?
- Do you usually have the same routine every day?
- Do you ever change your routine?
- Do you think it is important to have a daily routine?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: When do you usually get up in the morning?

Model answer: Mostly in situations that fit my routine. In my case, following a steady morning routine to stay focused.

#### Q2: Do you usually have the same routine every day?

Model answer: Yes, generally I would say so. For example, following a steady morning routine to stay focused.

#### Q3: Do you ever change your routine?

Model answer: Overall, yes, in most situations. A good example is following a steady morning routine to stay focused.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
