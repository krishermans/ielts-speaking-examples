This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of music. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Music

### Ideas Bank

- genres you enjoy most
- when you usually listen to music
- music in your school years
- live music vs recorded music
- the role of music in daily mood

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Do you like music?
- What is your favourite type of music?
- Did you learn music at school?
- Do you think music is important?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Do you like music?

Model answer: Overall, yes, in most situations. A good example is listening to playlists that match my mood or focus level.

#### Q2: What is your favourite type of music?

Model answer: I would describe it as practical and genuinely useful. Personally, listening to playlists that match my mood or focus level.

#### Q3: Did you learn music at school?

Model answer: In short, yes. In day-to-day life, listening to playlists that match my mood or focus level.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
