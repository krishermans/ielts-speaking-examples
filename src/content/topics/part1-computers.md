# Part 1 Computers

### Ideas Bank

- how often you use a computer
- main tasks you do online
- desktop vs laptop preferences
- digital skills in modern life
- screen-time balance

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Do you often use a computer?
- Do you prefer desktops or laptops?
- What do you use your computer for?
- Do you think it is important to learn how to use a computer?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Do you often use a computer?

Model answer: In short, yes. In day-to-day life, using my laptop daily for work, study, and planning.

#### Q2: Do you prefer desktops or laptops?

Model answer: Yes, generally I would say so. For example, using my laptop daily for work, study, and planning.

#### Q3: What do you use your computer for?

Model answer: I would describe it as practical and genuinely useful. Personally, using my laptop daily for work, study, and planning.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
