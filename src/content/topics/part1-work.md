This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of work. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Work

### Ideas Bank

- your current role or recent experience
- what you enjoy and what you find challenging
- work culture in your country
- changes in your priorities over time
- future plans and career direction

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- What is your job?
- Why did you choose that job?
- Do you like your job?
- Do you plan to continue with your job in the future?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: What is your job?

Model answer: I see it as a sensible choice rather than a trend. In my own routine, balancing deadlines and teamwork in my current role.

#### Q2: Why did you choose that job?

Model answer: Mainly because it gives me both stability and room to grow. Over time, I realized this option suits me best.

#### Q3: Do you like your job?

Model answer: Yes, generally I would say so. For example, balancing deadlines and teamwork in my current role.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
