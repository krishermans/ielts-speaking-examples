# Part 1 Dictionaries

### Ideas Bank

- paper vs digital dictionaries
- what information you look up most
- how dictionaries support language learning
- which type is most useful for learners
- how your dictionary habits changed

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Do you often use a dictionary?
- What do you use dictionaries for?
- What kinds of dictionaries are most useful?
- Do you think dictionaries are useful for learning a language?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Do you often use a dictionary?

Model answer: Yes, generally I would say so. For example, checking pronunciation and collocations when I study.

#### Q2: What do you use dictionaries for?

Model answer: I would describe it as practical and genuinely useful. Personally, checking pronunciation and collocations when I study.

#### Q3: What kinds of dictionaries are most useful?

Model answer: I would describe it as practical and genuinely useful. Personally, checking pronunciation and collocations when I study.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
