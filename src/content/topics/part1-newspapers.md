# Part 1 Newspapers

### Ideas Bank

- how you get your news
- print news vs digital news
- types of news you follow
- trust and reliability of sources
- importance of international news

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Do you often read newspapers?
- What kind of news do you usually follow?
- How do most people get the news in your country?
- Do you think international news is important?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Do you often read newspapers?

Model answer: Yes, generally I would say so. For example, checking headlines online and reading analysis later.

#### Q2: What kind of news do you usually follow?

Model answer: I would describe it as practical and genuinely useful. Personally, checking headlines online and reading analysis later.

#### Q3: How do most people get the news in your country?

Model answer: From my perspective, it is quite relevant in everyday life. For instance, checking headlines online and reading analysis later.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
