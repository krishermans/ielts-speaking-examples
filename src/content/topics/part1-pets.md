This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of pets. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Pets

### Ideas Bank

- whether you have or had a pet
- why people enjoy keeping pets
- daily responsibility of pet care
- popular pets in your country
- pets and companionship

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Do you have a pet?
- What is a popular pet in your country?
- Did you have a pet as a child?
- Why do people have pets?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Do you have a pet?

Model answer: In short, yes. In day-to-day life, seeing how daily pet care builds routine and responsibility.

#### Q2: What is a popular pet in your country?

Model answer: For me, it is both meaningful and realistic in daily life. A clear example is seeing how daily pet care builds routine and responsibility.

#### Q3: Did you have a pet as a child?

Model answer: Yes, generally I would say so. For example, seeing how daily pet care builds routine and responsibility.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
