This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of your home. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Home

### Ideas Bank

- house or flat and why it suits you
- favorite room and daily habits there
- what you would like to improve
- facilities in your neighborhood
- whether you plan to stay long-term

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Do you live in a house or a flat?
- What is your favourite room?
- What would you change about your home?
- What is your neighbourhood like?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Do you live in a house or a flat?

Model answer: Yes, generally I would say so. For example, spending most evenings in my living room to unwind.

#### Q2: What is your favourite room?

Model answer: For me, it is both meaningful and realistic in daily life. A clear example is spending most evenings in my living room to unwind.

#### Q3: What would you change about your home?

Model answer: For me, it is both meaningful and realistic in daily life. A clear example is spending most evenings in my living room to unwind.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
