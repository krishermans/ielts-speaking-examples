This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of reading. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Reading

### Ideas Bank

- what you like to read and why
- print books vs e-books
- childhood reading habits
- how often you read now
- ways to encourage children to read

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Do you often read?
- What is your favourite kind of book?
- What books did you read as a child?
- Is it important to encourage children to read?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Do you often read?

Model answer: In short, yes. In day-to-day life, reading short chapters in the evening to relax.

#### Q2: What is your favourite kind of book?

Model answer: I would describe it as practical and genuinely useful. Personally, reading short chapters in the evening to relax.

#### Q3: What books did you read as a child?

Model answer: I would describe it as practical and genuinely useful. Personally, reading short chapters in the evening to relax.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
