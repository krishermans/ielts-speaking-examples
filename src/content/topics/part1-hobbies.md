This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of hobbies. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Hobbies

### Ideas Bank

- your current hobby and why you chose it
- equipment or resources needed
- solo hobbies vs group hobbies
- childhood hobbies vs now
- how hobbies reduce stress

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Do you have a hobby?
- What equipment do you need for it?
- Did you have a hobby as a child?
- Why do you think people have hobbies?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Do you have a hobby?

Model answer: Overall, yes, in most situations. A good example is setting aside time each week for one personal hobby.

#### Q2: What equipment do you need for it?

Model answer: I would describe it as practical and genuinely useful. Personally, setting aside time each week for one personal hobby.

#### Q3: Did you have a hobby as a child?

Model answer: Overall, yes, in most situations. A good example is setting aside time each week for one personal hobby.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
