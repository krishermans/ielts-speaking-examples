# Part 1 Patience

### Ideas Bank

- situations where you are naturally patient
- triggers that make you impatient
- techniques to stay calm
- role models for patience
- why patience matters in daily life

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Are you a patient person?
- Do you ever get impatient?
- When was the last time you lost your patience?
- Which person in your family is the most patient?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Are you a patient person?

Model answer: Overall, yes, in most situations. A good example is staying calm in queues or when plans change unexpectedly.

#### Q2: Do you ever get impatient?

Model answer: Overall, yes, in most situations. A good example is staying calm in queues or when plans change unexpectedly.

#### Q3: When was the last time you lost your patience?

Model answer: Usually when it is convenient and easy to manage. For example, staying calm in queues or when plans change unexpectedly.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
