This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of weather. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Weather

### Ideas Bank

- weather you enjoy most and why
- how weather affects daily plans
- regional weather differences in your country
- transport disruption caused by weather
- seasonal preferences

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- What is the weather like today?
- What is your favourite weather?
- Does weather affect the way you feel?
- Does weather in your country affect transportation?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: What is the weather like today?

Model answer: I would describe it as practical and genuinely useful. Personally, adjusting my plans when it is very windy or rainy.

#### Q2: What is your favourite weather?

Model answer: For me, it is both meaningful and realistic in daily life. A clear example is adjusting my plans when it is very windy or rainy.

#### Q3: Does weather affect the way you feel?

Model answer: Overall, yes, in most situations. A good example is adjusting my plans when it is very windy or rainy.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
