This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of birthdays. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Birthdays

### Ideas Bank

- how you usually celebrate
- a memorable birthday experience
- important milestone ages in your culture
- family traditions
- how birthday celebrations change with age

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Do you usually celebrate your birthdays?
- How did you celebrate your last birthday?
- Which birthdays are the most important ones in your country?
- Do you think children should celebrate their birthdays with a party?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Do you usually celebrate your birthdays?

Model answer: Yes, generally I would say so. For example, celebrating with a small family dinner rather than a big party.

#### Q2: How did you celebrate your last birthday?

Model answer: To me, it is clearly part of daily life. A simple example is celebrating with a small family dinner rather than a big party.

#### Q3: Which birthdays are the most important ones in your country?

Model answer: I would describe it as practical and genuinely useful. Personally, celebrating with a small family dinner rather than a big party.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
