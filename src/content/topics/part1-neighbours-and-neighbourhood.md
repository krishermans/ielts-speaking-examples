# Part 1 Neighbours And Neighbourhood

### Ideas Bank

- how well you know your neighbours
- what makes a neighbourhood pleasant
- safety and local facilities
- community spirit in your area
- how your area could improve

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Do you like your neighbours?
- What is your neighbourhood like?
- How could your neighbourhood be improved?
- Is it important to have a good relationship with neighbours?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Do you like your neighbours?

Model answer: Yes, generally I would say so. For example, greeting neighbours and using nearby facilities daily.

#### Q2: What is your neighbourhood like?

Model answer: For me, it is both meaningful and realistic in daily life. A clear example is greeting neighbours and using nearby facilities daily.

#### Q3: How could your neighbourhood be improved?

Model answer: I would say it matters more than people think. In practical terms, greeting neighbours and using nearby facilities daily.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
