# Part 1 Leisure Time

- active vs passive leisure
- alone time vs social time
- weekend routine
- importance of work-life balance

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- What is your favourite leisure activity?
- Do you prefer free time with other people or alone?
- Do most people in your country get two days off a week?
- Do you think leisure time is important?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: What is your favourite leisure activity?

Model answer: I would describe it as practical and genuinely useful. Personally, using free time for relaxing activities that clear my mind.

#### Q2: Do you prefer free time with other people or alone?

Model answer: Overall, yes, in most situations. A good example is using free time for relaxing activities that clear my mind.

#### Q3: Do most people in your country get two days off a week?

Model answer: Yes, generally I would say so. For example, using free time for relaxing activities that clear my mind.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
