This study note helps you prepare IELTS Speaking Part 1 answers for the topic of going out. Keep your answers personal, direct, and natural.

# Part 1 Going Out

### Ideas Bank

- how often you go out socially
- preferred places and activities
- going out alone vs with friends
- budget-friendly options
- local nightlife trends

### Part 1 Prompt Starters

- Do you often go out in the evenings?
- What do you like to do when you go out?
- Do you prefer going out on your own or with friends?
- Where do most young people like to go out in your country?

### Sample Short Questions & Model Answers

#### Q1: Do you often go out in the evenings?

Model answer: Yes, generally I would say so. For example, meeting friends for dinner or live music on weekends.

#### Q2: What do you like to do when you go out?

Model answer: For me, it is both meaningful and realistic in daily life. A clear example is meeting friends for dinner or live music on weekends.

#### Q3: Do you prefer going out on your own or with friends?

Model answer: Yes, generally I would say so. For example, meeting friends for dinner or live music on weekends.

### Study Tips

- Give one short personal example in each answer.
- Use 1 to 2 paraphrases naturally instead of repeating the same words.
- Keep each answer around two to four sentences so it sounds natural in the test.
